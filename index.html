Places my work has taken me and the projects I've worked on


PROJECTS

BalloonSat

Gateway to Space Semester Project, Fall 2015

Designed, proposed, built, and tested a balloon-sat payload to test different shielding materials in a high altitude environment.

Sun Tracking Solar Panel

Electronics for the Physical Sciences Semester Project, Fall 2016

Designed, built, and tested an analog circuit for a miniature solar tracking solar panel array.

Guitar Signal Analysis

Fourier Series and Boundary Value Problems Semester Project, Fall 2016

Modeled the oscillations of a guitar string using three variations to the 1D wave equation.

Mass-Radius Relation for Stable Neutron Stars

Gravitational Theory Term Paper, Spring 2019

Developed a numerical solver for the coupled Tolman-Oppenheimer-Volkov equations to determine themass-radius relationship for stable neutron stars.

Neutrinoless Double Beta Decay

Nuclear and Particle Physics Term Paper, Spring 2018

Conducted a detailed literature review of the theoretical neutrinoless double beta decay mechanism –outlining the historical and physical context, the theoretical mechanism, and the ongoing experimentalresearch of the topic.

Hydrogen Cyclotron

Introductory Plasma Physics Final Project, Fall 2017

Used the Runge-Kutta method (RK4) to derive the motions of a hydrogen atom in a cyclotron under different driving frequencies.

Nelder Mead Minimization Method

Modeling in Applied Math Semester Project, Fall 2018

Programmed a generic Nelder-Mead simplex search algorithm which was used to model the detectorresponsivity of the DAXSS instrument.
